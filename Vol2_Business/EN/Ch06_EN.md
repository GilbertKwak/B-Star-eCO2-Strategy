## 6.2 KGS AC117 Key Requirements

- Maximum allowable pressure: 130 bar (safety factor ≥1.5)
- CO₂ leak detection: Auto shut-off at concentration >1%LEL
- Pressure relief valves: Dual PRV installation (main + backup)
- Refrigerant charge: Single fill <150 kg (small system exemption)

## 6.3 Fire Safety Compliance

- CO₂ density >1.5 kg/m³ detection triggers automatic exhaust fan
- Installation: Ventilation system interlocking required (KBC 608.4)
- Emergency exit signs and evacuation alarms required

## 6.4 Hazard Assessment (HAZOP)

| Deviation | Cause | Consequence | Protection Layers (LOPA) |
|---|---|---|---|
| High pressure | Expansion valve failure | PCHE rupture | PRV + ESD + pressure alarm |
| Low temperature | Refrigerant overcharge | Pipe embrittlement | Temp alarm + auto shutoff |
| Leak | Flange seal failure | CO₂ asphyxiation | Gas detector + forced ventilation |
