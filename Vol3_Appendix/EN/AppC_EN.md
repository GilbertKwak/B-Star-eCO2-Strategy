## C-1. Glossary

| Term | Abbreviation | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| Supercritical Carbon Dioxide | sCO₂ | CO₂ refrigerant operating above critical point (31.1°C, 7.38MPa); simultaneously exhibits gas and liquid properties |
| Coefficient of Performance | COP | Cooling output (kW) ÷ power consumption (kW). B★ design target: 7.4 |
| Printed Circuit Heat Exchanger | PCHE | Ultra-high-efficiency plate heat exchanger manufactured by chemical etching |
| Technology Readiness Level | TRL | Scale from 1 (basic principles) to 9 (proven in operational environment) |
| Power Usage Effectiveness | PUE | Total DC power ÷ IT equipment power. Closer to 1.0 = more efficient |
| Measurement, Reporting & Verification | MRV | Quantitative verification framework for greenhouse gas reduction performance |
| Deep Reinforcement Learning | DRL | AI algorithm applied to sCO₂ compressor surge prevention control |
| Life Cycle Assessment | LCA | Environmental impact assessment across entire product lifecycle (production to disposal) |
| Net-Zero | Net-Zero | State where greenhouse gas emissions equal absorption, resulting in zero net emissions |
| Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures | TCFD | Framework for climate risk financial disclosure |
| Energy Saving Contract | ESC | Contract model where solution provider shares a portion of energy savings achieved |
| Operating Expenditure | OPEX | Recurring costs such as maintenance and subscriptions |
| Capital Expenditure | CAPEX | One-time investment costs such as system purchase and installation |
| Weighted Average Cost of Capital | WACC | Discount rate used in DCF. B★ standard: 12% |
| Internal Rate of Return | IRR | B★ Series A basis: 42.4% (investor, 5-year) |
| Net Present Value | NPV | B★ WACC 12% basis: $81.7M |
| Korea Gas Safety Corporation | KGS | Certification authority for CO₂ refrigerant equipment under KGS AC117 |
